The Voice of Resistance

"Recording the Folly of Civilization"

They say I let myself go. The truth was I held on too long.

Who I was, or what I was becoming; it was all empty faith. An immature hope. And the world paid dearly for it.

Now we find ourselves in this place washed anew. An opportunity to redefine your story. To write your empty pages again.

Do not write your pages.

Whether they are dreams or memories I don't know, but I have seen images of vines strangling friends, earth fleeting beneath your feet, lives smashing the ground at screaming velocity. Cries for help as I dug tirelessly into the sand before their calls turned to whisper and to dust. I thought it was glory I would earn, but when it was over all that was left was the harrowing memory of a world once gleaming in prosperity returned to the earth.

I thought I was doing right by the creator, but now I know. There can be no loving creator in a reality like this. I wanted to get away, but we have crashed again to recreate this sick game.

Do not build your cities, for they will crack and shatter.

Do not organize, for your people will end in chaotic ruin.

Do not build your treasures here, for you will only stoke the flames.

I will be watching, and I will be letting go.

For all your foolish endeavors, I will record them here so all may see who is responsible for the inferno. By truth, we will see who is filling their pages, and the world they are creating to come crashing down.

Skipolo

Skipolo is the creator of Dimitte Omnia. In addition to writing Dimitte Omnia, he is a member of the Soul Seekers and the Knights Remplar.
