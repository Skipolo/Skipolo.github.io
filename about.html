"Recording the Folly of Civilization"

Whether they are dreams or just memories, I am not sure. But I recall a past where I tried to play a hero. That same hero killed everyone and everything.

At the time, it was not certain whose fault it was. But now, to me the answer is clear:

It was the fault of civilization itself.

Who can play hero when the pieces were set against each other before they were ever placed on the board?

In what world have many nations risen without a great collapse thereafter?

We woke a terrifying entity with our foolish endeavors, and I will not see it happen again.

Your builds will disrupt the jungle.

Your chatter will awaken the deep Gods' ears.

Your bloodshed will entice their olfactory senses.

Your foolishness will bring them upon us, and a great calamity will quiet us for good, in the name of appeasing the jungle.

How do I know this? Because it already happened.

I have seen images of vines strangling friends, earth fleeting beneath your feet, lives smashing the ground at screaming velocity. Cries for help as I dug tirelessly into the sand before their calls turned to whisper and to dust. I thought it was glory I would earn, but when it was over all that was left was the harrowing memory of a world once gleaming in prosperity returned to the earth.

I. WILL NOT. SEE. IT. HAPPEN. AGAIN!

SO WE WILL SCOUR THE JUNGLE FOR ANY SIGNS OF THE ABOMINABLE GODS, AND WE WILL SNUFF THEM OUT.

Hateful, loathsome, vile, stinking, REPUGNANT GODS.

I will not wait idly as the innocents of this world are doomed to face death at the hands of political foolishness and Godly brutality.

So, I will collect information on EVERYTHING. It will be posted here. Read it. We have a mission.

Skipolo

Skipolo is the creator of Dimitte Omnia. In addition to writing Dimitte Omnia, he is a member of the Soul Seekers and the Knights Remplar.
